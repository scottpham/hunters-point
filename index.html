Hunters Point

The decommissioned Hunters Point Naval Shipyard includes a federal superfund site that includes about 800 acres of land and water. For years, the Navy has been decontaminating the site for eventual transfer to the city of San Francisco. The area is divided into parcels, most of which have not yet been fully cleaned and transferred.
